St Sidwells Point

Opening in 2022, SSP is the first leisure complex in the UK to be built to the ultra energy efficient Passivhaus standard.

Parking

Bampfylde Street car park is opposite the complex, however this car park can often be full at training times. The Triangle car park is a few minutes walk away and always has spaces. Parking is free in both spots after 6pm. There is plenty of undercover bicycle parking by the complex entrance.
